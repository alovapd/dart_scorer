    Help & FAQ

    Dart Scorer Pro is a premium upgrade that unlocks advanced features including dart sounds, voice announcer, detailed game statistics, improvement insights, turn history, cross-device game sync, and customizable preferences.

    The free version includes the full dart scorer for solo practice with all game types (X01, Cricket, Around the Clock). Creating a free account unlocks multiplayer, vs virtual opponents, and joining games with friends.

    When you upgrade to Pro, you get a 7-day free trial with full access to all Pro features. A payment method is required to start the trial, but you won't be charged until the trial ends. If you cancel before the 7 days are up, you won't be charged at all.

    To switch plans, cancel your current subscription first. You'll keep Pro access until the end of your current billing period. Then simply re-subscribe on your preferred plan. Your stats and account data are always preserved.

    You can cancel your subscription at any time. If you're on a trial, you won't be charged. If you're on a paid plan, you'll keep Pro access until the end of your current billing period.
